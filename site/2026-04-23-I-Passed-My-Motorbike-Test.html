"I passed my motorbike test"

2026-04-23

Last year I got my Learner motorbike licnese. Six months after that I actually got a motorbike to ride. A week ago I went for my Provisional riders test to get my "Red P's". And I passed! Yay!

Honestly felt a little unprepaired for it as no one I knew had done one recently. I live in regional NSW Australia and therefore Im in an "unserviced" area. This essentially means I dont have to do any of the required motorcycle courses and can just do a simple kerb side test at the local ServiceNSW centre. This test involved a few tasks including:

  • 4 left turns around the block ServiceNSW centre
  • 4 right turns
  • a U-turn
  • a Emergency break
  • a 20m crawl at 5-7km/h

I nailed everything but the crawl and jeez it was close. Never had to move so slow for so long and it was a struggle to stay upright. But despite my wobbling I thankfully passed. :)
